Dark Magician Girl over Pixie

P4G mod by Germaniac
04/04/2021

First release, just to get it out there. To be used with Aemulus.

Current learnset:
Innate - Spell Master
Innate - Zio
Innate - Tarukaja
14 - Mazio
21 - Zionga
28 - Megido
35 - Mazionga
40 - Elec Boost
44 - Matarukaja
47 - Ziodyne
50 - Mind Charge
54 - Makarakarn
59 - Megidola
62 - Maziodyne
66 - Megidolaon
72 - Elec Amp

Note that you have to get her card from Shuffle Time again to get the new innate skills.

It's designed with the intent of making her a Persona you can get early and keep for a long time by mimicking team member's Personas, though unfortunately the game limits Persona learnset at 16. If you want to bring back Pixie's learnset and affinities, delete PERSONA_Patches.tbp (for learnset) and UNIT_Patches.tbp (for affinities) in the tblpatches folder.

Things to do:
- Balancing stats/learnset
- Moving her to an unused Persona slot
- Polishing in general

Escape Route Fuuka Always Available

P3FES mod by Suit
03/28/2021

This is mainly for players who have already beaten the game and are either playing a New Game+ or are starting The Answer. Since you have to level up Fuuka again for escape route, I've decided to make it available from the start, since leaving Tartarus or a door in The Answer is always up to luck. This cheat does not account for Mitsuru, since she is never supposed to learn this skill, so she does not have extra choices in Tartarus, like music, unlike Fuuka. You also can't check Mitsuru's skills when talking to her, so it would seem that some modding would have to be done to get this idea to work.

Epic Partypanel In Color -Royal Edition-

P5R mod by Century_
03/26/2021 (updated)

Updated 03/26/2021
The P5R partypanel was lacking some major drip, so I made this mod that revamps the P5R character sprites for much more EPIC colored variants. It comes in both a normal version and an uncompressed version. The uncompressed variant contains fewer artifacts than the normal one, but it will be heavier on the game's memory which means that it might crash in certain instances. (Requires more testing)

Of course, big mcthankies from mcspankies to Hanakomisa for testing.
