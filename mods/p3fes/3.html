P3 Movie Music FES P3FES Mod Sate 04/25/2021 (updated)


Download

Tags:
BGM 		Replaces the game's music with songs from all four of the Persona 3 movies. Broadly speaking, they correspond to the same situations from the movies. (i.e. Persona Summoners plays while fighting Tartarus minibosses, since it played while fighting a Tartarus miniboss in the first movie)

Leftover tracks use the high quality versions from my conversion/expansion of this game's HQ music mod for Portable on here, to clear up any confusion.
