P3P: The Shadow Hour

P3P mod by Augmented Antics
2/2/2021 (updated)

UPDATE (2/1/2021):
- Corrected some typos in the changes.txt
- Corrected affinities of some Red Shadows that were missed
- Included an Anti-Tedium Add-On that's meant to mitigate some of the boring, needless grinding, particularly for money and materials. Details on what was changed is included in the changes.txt.
Welcome to P3P: The Shadow Hour! An issue I've always had with this game was how your party always seemed to outclass the enemies, which I would assume is because the game wasn't rebalanced with the new features in mind. This mod attempts to rectify this, at least somewhat, by making the enemies retain their competition against you throughout the game.

Modern Skill Rebalance

P3P mod by Phoenix!
01/17/2021

What is this?
This mod alters the attributes of many (82!) moves in Persona 3 to be more representative of their Persona 4/5 versions. 

• v1.1 MSR now affects both The Journey and The Answer.
• v1.2 incorporates the Skill Backport mod, along with some addition changes.
• v1.25 adds Blazing Hell.

The original thumbnail was by the wonderful hanakomisa on the Henry-C discord.
