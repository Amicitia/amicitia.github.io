Improved Naoto Social Link

P4G mod by MadMax1960
06/23/2021

The main purpose of this mod is to give Naoto Social Link a better feel and flow for her romance Route. The changes that have been made, while small, are extremely impactful on giving more control to Naoto in her actions instead of being essentially being manipulated into them by the main protagonist. 

Every change for this mod is OPTIONAL, and different versions are available to suit peoples preferences.

Romance Starting Dialogue 
While the starting Romance dialogue "I like that you're a girl." Is fine, doesn't really match Yu's character, and is just a bit of a weird thing to say in the moment. 

The change done here reinforces the overall message of gender being irrelevant to Naoto's skill as a detective and fits the scene better. In order to do this I basically just flipped the second option to the first option so it's the new romance flag. I adjusted her responses to this afterward to make it more directed at Yu. After-Battle Ammo Refill

P5 mod by chlorophylls
06/02/2021 (updated)

Updated 06/02/2021
This mod makes it so that your ammo automatically replenishes after every battle, just like how it works in P5R.

Also adjusts the tutorial messages and cutscene to reflect the change.
For Mod Compendium
As A-NB has noted, this may conflict with other mods that replace dngPack.pac in field/, such as the Mod Menu. If that happens, use Amicitia to replace the support.bf file in that dngPack.pac and atDngPack.pac with the one in this mod (in the "BF Script" folder).

This mod is not compatible with the Afterbattle Replenish Mod.

Original thread on Amicitia forums
