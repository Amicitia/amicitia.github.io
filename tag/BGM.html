P3 Movie Music FES

P3FES mod by Sate
04/25/2021 (updated)

Updated 04/25/2021
Replaces the game's music with songs from all four of the Persona 3 movies. Broadly speaking, they correspond to the same situations from the movies. (i.e. Persona Summoners plays while fighting Tartarus minibosses, since it played while fighting a Tartarus miniboss in the first movie)

This also has some artistic license just for fun, like Tartarus and The Abyss of Time's themes being replaced with music from the movies based on whoever will be important during that block. Leftover tracks use the high quality versions from my conversion/expansion of this game's HQ music mod for Portable on here, to clear up any confusion. It also "adds" a song via messing with an ambient sound loops (the night trip to Gekkoukan has music now)
