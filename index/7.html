Slim Font P4G Mod Pixelguin 05/31/2021 (updated)


Download

Tags:
Font UI 		Persona 4 Golden has a wider font than the PS2 version of Persona 4, presumably to make the text easier to read on the small PS Vita screen. However, it's much too large for a PC monitor or even a television screen.

This mod replaces P4G's font with the slimmer Skip Std-B font, slightly edited to fit texture height limits. Each character was individually typed and spaced using Photoshop and PersonaEditor, which also fixes some overlapping glyph issues from the original font.
