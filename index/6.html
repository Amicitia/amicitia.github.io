After-Battle Ammo Refill

P5 mod by chlorophylls
06/02/2021 (updated)

Updated 06/02/2021
This mod makes it so that your ammo automatically replenishes after every battle, just like how it works in P5R.

Also adjusts the tutorial messages and cutscene to reflect the change.
For Mod Compendium
As A-NB has noted, this may conflict with other mods that replace dngPack.pac in field/, such as the Mod Menu. If that happens, use Amicitia to replace the support.bf file in that dngPack.pac and atDngPack.pac with the one in this mod (in the "BF Script" folder).

This mod is not compatible with the Afterbattle Replenish Mod.

Slim Font

P4G mod by Pixelguin
05/31/2021 (updated)

Updated 05/31/2021
Persona 4 Golden has a wider font than the PS2 version of Persona 4, presumably to make the text easier to read on the small PS Vita screen. However, it's much too large for a PC monitor or even a television screen.

This mod replaces P4G's font with the slimmer Skip Std-B font, slightly edited to fit texture height limits. Each character was individually typed and spaced using Photoshop and PersonaEditor, which also fixes some overlapping glyph issues from the original font.
