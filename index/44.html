When P4G was ported to PC, I knew that modding would run rampant. One of my favorite videos of all time is "Persona 420 Mod - Download in Description", and I remember seeing it get ported to the Vita. I had the feeling that someone would port it to PC, but I never imagined it would be me. There were two main issues with porting this in the first place. The first is that the pac files didn't actually change Yosuke at all, meaning I had to manually copy every single texture into literal hundreds of folders. In order to do that in the first place, I had to extract the TM2 files from all of the AMDs (which was quite a few), convert them to PNG, convert those PNGs to DDS, then copy the DDS' into the different folders.
